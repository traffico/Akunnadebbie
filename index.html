About Pictures of Akunna Videos

Just taking some time out to do this for someone special to me. My name is Triumphant Ogidi a corp member in Eket, Akwa Ibom State. I would like to say a few things about a wonderful person I met. Her name is Akunna Ogochukwu Deborah. She reminds of the song Hakuna Matata from the popular movie, “The Lion King”. I've known her for quite some time but it feels as though I've known her for a very long time. During the last three weeks of her stay in Eket we bonded well

She calls me Trumpet and I call her Debbie, Debra, Akunna, Hakuna, My Debbie, in fact anything sweet that comes to mind. How funny it was for someone to change my birthname to something as hilarious as trumpet.
I have always been ok with it… you know why???? I won't tell. Surprisingly others started calling me what she calls me……

Facts about Akunna

  • She's purposeful, she knows what she wants and works towards it
  • She's not affected by what people thinks about her
  • She's strong willed
  • She cares… I've seen that myself
  • She loves to sing just like I do and also plays music with her music player which I wasn't able to see (she understands this part)
  • She's a giver
  • She's quite stubborn though, I love that anyways
  • She loves God

There is a lot to say about Debbie

I almost forgot this part; she knows how to dance. To be honest, her dance steps makes me smile. She's also playful, very playful and a good cook as well, I call her chef. She makes the best chin-chin I've ever tasted. The members of Nccf Eket can attest to it.

I remember this day I was playing the piano and was singing a song. I don’t remember the title but I'll give a few lines of the song

“Decisions must be made
But now I have a choice
I need your wisdom Lord
I need to hear your voice…”

Yeah, that's the song. She came from her room to join me sing that song. And each time I would want her to come out, I would simply sing the song and play the piano along which she finds hard to resist. The song is quite emotional since it reminds her of a bad experience which she conquered because she is a strong woman.

She has a cool way of calling somethings which got me flummoxed most of the time. She calls egusi egs, mosquitoes quitoes, cockroaches roaches etc. I can't remember all of it right now… There's a lot to say about this wonderful person God brought my way

I was hurt when she decided to return back home, but I’m positive I’ll meet her again so we get to carry out our plans of going swimming, making a singing video together, getting to start an argument that would attract attention, teaching her how to play chess etc.……

She knows I miss her so much and I know she misses me too… I’ll always cherish the moments we had together. She knows how much I love and cherish her. You will always be in my heart Akunna

Lovely
